My Journey

From a Needle & Thread
To a Beloved Boutique

Hi, I am Anushka Gupta — a home-based fashion designer and master tailor based in Mumbai. What started as a childhood passion for stitching has grown into a full boutique practice over the past 5 years.

I started Anushka Boutique from my home in Mumbai with a simple belief: every woman deserves clothing that fits perfectly and feels beautiful. I offer something different — something personal.

Today I stitch kurtis, corsets, sharara, palazzo pants, skirts, lehengas, crop tops, bodycon dresses, baby frocks, satin bows, face masks — and anything you can dream of through a custom order. Every piece is stitched by me personally with care and precision.
