Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How do I share my measurements?

After placing your order, Anushka will send a WhatsApp message with a measurement guide. You can share your measurements via text, or she can guide you step by step on a quick call.

How long does stitching take?

Standard orders: 5–7 days. Embroidery/custom work: 7–14 days. Bridal lehenga: 15–20 days. Delivery time varies by location. Anushka will confirm your timeline at the time of order.

Can I provide my own fabric?

Absolutely! Many customers send their own fabric. Anushka can also help you source quality fabric from her trusted suppliers based on your design requirements and budget.

Do you do alterations?

Yes! Anushka does alterations for garments she's stitched as well as existing clothes you'd like resized or restyled. Contact her with details and photos for a quick quote.

What categories do you stitch?

Kurtis, blouses, corsets, sharara, palazzo pants, skirts, lehengas, crop tops, bodycon dresses, baby frocks, satin bows, face masks, and any custom design you have in mind!

How do I pay?

Payment is arranged via WhatsApp after your order is confirmed. Anushka accepts UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), bank transfer, and cash on delivery for Mumbai customers.
